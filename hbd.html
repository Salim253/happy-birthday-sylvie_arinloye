Dear Aunt,

On this magnificent day that the Lord has created especially for you, my heart overflows with gratitude. I pray that God will bless you with even stronger health, fill your soul with His grace, and allow you to taste the sweet fruits of all that you have sown with such love.

Thank you for being the extraordinary aunt you are: a comforting presence, a beautiful example to follow, and endless generosity to me and my brothers.

You are a ray of sunshine in my life. Know that I love you very much, sincerely and deeply. I will do everything not to disappoint you and to bring you the joy you so richly deserve.

May the Almighty bless you abundantly and may this new year lead you from glory to glory, ever more radiant. In the precious name of Jesus Christ. Amen!

Your loving son.

With all my love
